Jyothi Boutique is a modern e-commerce website designed for a boutique specializing in **Thanjavur Paintings**, custom embroidery, and artisanal handcrafted gifts. The platform allows customers to explore beautifully crafted traditional Indian art, view product details, and easily connect with the boutique for orders and inquiries.

The website provides a clean and responsive user interface, ensuring a smooth browsing experience across desktops and mobile devices. Customers can browse collections, view services offered by the boutique, and place inquiries or orders through integrated WhatsApp and contact features.

This project highlights a professional boutique presence online while showcasing traditional craftsmanship with a modern digital shopping experience.
